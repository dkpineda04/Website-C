About Luca

Hi — I’m Luca. Baking has been my dream since childhood. After a few detours in life — including a stint as a lifeguard — I followed my hands and heart to baking. Luca Loaves began in Sydney CBD to bring slow, intentional bread back to the neighbourhood.

Why our sourdough?

  • No commercial yeast — only natural starter
  • Hand-kneaded & shaped in-house
  • Long fermentation (14–17 hours) for flavour & digestibility
  • Organic flour, filtered water, and a solar-powered oven

Mission

We make real bread from the best organic ingredients — all by hand, with warmth and care, and a dash of patience.
